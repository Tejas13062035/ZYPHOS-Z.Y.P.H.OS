**Historical Significance**

In the 19th century, John Wesley Jarvis was a renowned American painter and portrait artist. By 1814, he had risen to the top of his profession, taking on an unprecedented commission for six full-length portraits of the naval heroes of the War of 1812. His work showcased his exceptional talent and skill.

**Notable Deaths**

Gregory Bruce Jarvis, an American engineer and astronaut, tragically died on January 28, 1986, during the destruction of the Space Shuttle Challenger. His legacy as a dedicated astronaut and engineer lives on.

**Legal Precedents**
